Authorization & Ethics

All security services provided by PulseSec follow strict ethical and legal standards.

Explicit Authorization

Every security assessment, simulation or penetration test is performed only with written authorization from the client and within a clearly defined scope.

Controlled Testing

All tests are designed to minimize operational risk and never interfere with productive business processes.

Responsible Disclosure

Identified vulnerabilities are reported confidentially and responsibly, including clear recommendations for remediation.

PulseSec does not perform unauthorized testing, exploit misuse or data exfiltration.
